Core idea

TTC(n) is defined as a global toroidal C1 carrier with an internal Cn structure and a discrete monodromy. The central case TTC(6) uses a 60° step and returns the internal orientation after six phase cycles.

Responsible status

The model is formally/geometrically structured. Magnetic, inductive or material extensions are explicitly conditional and require simulation, FEM modeling and experimental measurement.

Compact formula

TTC(n) = global C1 torus + internal Cn section + monodromy R(2π/n)
MTTC(n) = TTC(n) + magnetic distribution compatible with R(2π/n)
PMTTC(n) = physical / prototypal implementation of MTTC(n)
