Abstract

TSC(n) — Spherical Cyclic Twist — is proposed as a deterministic framework for constructing and classifying closed cyclic loops on a sphere. The central relation is the component-count criterion Ncomp = gcd(s,m), obtained from modular reconnection orbits.

Notă metodologică

Lucrarea este publicată ca model geometric, topologic și combinatoric. Direcțiile fizice, magnetice sau constructive sunt tratate ca extensii aplicative ulterioare, nu ca validări experimentale.
